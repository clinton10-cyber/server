How it works

  • Both scripts are only loaded in the browser after a visitor accepts the cookie consent banner.
  • AdSense units placed via Ads render inside the ad slots on the homepage and article pages once accepted.
  • Mellowtel requires its own separate opt-in per their terms — the same accept action covers both here, but you can split them if you want more granular consent.
  • To change these values, edit your .env file and restart the app.
